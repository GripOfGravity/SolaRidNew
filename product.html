Product and Capabilities

Insect Attractants

Our device provides users the opportunity to pick-and-choose a selection of up to five interchangeable attractants, ranging from variable light frequencies, variable sound frequencies, and semiochemical / kairomone pheromone-based attractants.

These attractants, when used together in an effective manner, provide our data-processing AI software with the most accurate count of insects in your field, providing the user with the most accurate insect population information.

IIoT Functions and Capabilities

External Thermistor Measures ambient temperatures
Humidity Sensors
 Measures and displays humidity levels
Cell Modem Module Stores data in an SQL database
Strike Detection Detects voltage reduction and counts numbers of insect strikes hourly
Software Controlled Adjustments Used to turn Killing Grid system ON / OFF and to adjust Voltage Levels remotely
Precipitation Detection Detects rainfall and shuts off Killing Grid
Firmware Updates Internet downloaded updates for the machine

Device Sensors

Our device also provides many sensors, allowing users to receive real-time updates on a variety of aspects that may potentially affect the productivity of your fields and the rate at which insects affect them. The sensors include:

  • Insect Species / Population
  • Soil Moisture / Precipitation
  • Temperature
  • Humidity
  • Wind Speed / Direction
